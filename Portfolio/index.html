About Me

Hi! I’m Amelia Donhauser, a multimedia journalist originally from New York City and currently a senior at Northwestern University’s Medill School of Journalism. I’m pursuing a double major in journalism and history, with a concentration in the Americas.

I’ve interned for CBS News, where I fact-checked speeches during the Democratic National Convention, verified footage for major broadcasts and analyzed misinformation trends on social media platforms. On campus, I write, edit and produce stories for WNUR News, the local radio news station.

I am passionnate about telling stories through writing, podcasting and photography. I’ve contributed to the podcast “Making Gay History” and pitched documentary ideas while interning for Emmy-nominated filmmaker Lisa Cortés.

Thanks for visiting — feel free to explore my writing, photography and multimedia work.
